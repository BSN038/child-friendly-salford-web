About Child Friendly Salford

Child Friendly Salford is part of UNICEF UK’s Child Friendly Cities and Communities programme. The programme supports councils and partners to put children’s rights into practice, ensuring children and young people are heard, supported and valued in their local area.

This website provides a central place for families, young people, organisations and partners to learn about the programme, access updates and find ways to get involved.
