Families & Young People

This section is for children, young people and families. It will provide clear information, helpful links and ways to take part in Child Friendly Salford.

What this programme means

Child Friendly Salford works to ensure children’s rights are understood and supported locally, and that young people have meaningful opportunities to share their views.

Get involved

You can take part by sharing feedback, joining activities and following updates. We’ll add new opportunities here as the programme develops.
